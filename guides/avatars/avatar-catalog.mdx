---
title: "Using Public Avatars"
description: "Browse and use public avatars in your scenarios."
---

Akapulu provides a public catalog of avatars you can use in your conversations.

We add new public avatars regularly, so your available options continue to grow over time.

<Note>
If you need a custom avatar (for example custom backgrounds or other specific details), see our [Enterprise plan](https://akapulu.com/pricing).
</Note>


You can view the public catalog at [akapulu.com/catalog](https://akapulu.com/catalog).

Click on any avatar row to view its details

Each catalog avatar includes a handle, profile image, description, and a sample video of what this avatar looks and sounds like

<img src="/images/guides/avatars/use-public-avatar/catalog-avatar-demo.png" alt="Public avatar catalog showing handle, profile image, description, and sample video" style={{ borderRadius: 12 }} />

Public avatar handles use the `public_*` prefix.

When creating a scenario, open the **Avatar** tab and select any public catalog avatar.
