<Warning>
Self-serve avatar creation is deprecated and no longer available.
</Warning>

Custom private avatars are now provisioned by the Akapulu Labs team as part of the [Enterprise plan](https://akapulu.com/pricing).

If you want a custom private avatar that looks and sounds like you (or your brand), join Enterprise and we'll build it with you. Reach out at [contact@akapulu.com](mailto:contact@akapulu.com) to get started.

In the meantime, you can use any avatar from our [public catalog](/guides/avatars/avatar-catalog) in your conversations.
